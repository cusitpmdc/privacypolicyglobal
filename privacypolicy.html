Global College ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting any information you access through our mobile application.

This Privacy Policy explains how the Global College app works, especially in relation to user data.

1. No Data Collection

The Global College app does not collect, store, or share any personal data from users. It functions purely as a secure access point for students to view academic and administrative information provided by their educational institution.

2. How the App Works

  • The app connects to systems managed by your college or university.
  • All personal and academic data displayed in the app (such as class schedules, exam results, attendance, etc.) is fetched from and stored by your institution, not by us.
  • We do not collect or process this information ourselves, nor do we share it with any third parties.

3. Third-Party Access

We do not integrate third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking tools. Your information is not shared with external services.

4. Data Security

While we do not store any user data, we are committed to ensuring that all access to your institution’s systems is secure. We use encryption and secure communication protocols to protect your session during use.

5. Your Institution’s Responsibility

Your data (including exam results, attendance, and fee details) is managed by your educational institution. For any questions or requests regarding your personal or academic data, please contact your institution directly.

6. Children's Privacy

This app is intended for use by students of educational institutions. If you are under the age of 13, please use this app only with parental or institutional guidance.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in the app or legal requirements. Updated versions will be posted at the same URL with a new "Effective Date."
